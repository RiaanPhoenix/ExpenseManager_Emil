# ExpenseManager Emil

> **A premium Icelandic spending insight app that turns bank transactions into clear, useful, and actionable financial understanding.**

---

## What It Is

A personal finance web app for people in Iceland who want to understand their spending habits, manage expenses better, and feel more in control of their money.

The core question the app answers:

> "Where is my money actually going?"

This is not a budgeting tool. It is a **personal spending insight app** — built for Icelanders, priced for the Icelandic economy, and designed to feel trustworthy, calm, and premium.

---

## Target Users

| Segment | Description |
|---|---|
| **Individual** | Earns decent money, but doesn't know where it goes. Wants clarity without manual tracking. |
| **Household** | Couples sharing rent, groceries, cars, subscriptions. Need a shared financial view. |
| **Freelancer** | Needs to separate personal and business expenses. Prepares for taxes. Exports to accountant. |

## Key Features

### Dashboard
- Total spending this month
- Income vs. spending
- Biggest category
- Month-over-month comparison
- Upcoming recurring payments
- Unusual spending alerts
- Savings opportunities

### Spending Categories
Auto-categorized: Groceries, Restaurants, Fuel, Housing, Utilities, Subscriptions, Shopping, Health, Fitness, Travel, Entertainment, Kids/Family, Car, Insurance, Loans, Income, Transfers

### Merchant Insights
Understand spending at the merchant level — not just categories.
- Top merchants this month
- Most frequent merchants
- Biggest increases
- "You visited cafés 18 times this month"

### Recurring Payments
Detects and lists all recurring payments (Netflix, Spotify, gym, phone, insurance, loans, etc.)
- Monthly + yearly totals
- "Possibly unused" labels
- Next payment dates

### Soft Budgets
Non-punishing budget tracking.
- Progress bars per category
- Calm alerts: *"You are 82% through your restaurant budget with 11 days left."*

### Monthly Report
Auto-generated end-of-month summary:
- Total income/spending/savings
- Biggest categories and merchants
- Unusual transactions
- Suggested focus for next month
- PDF export (Premium)

### Household Mode
Shared financial view for couples/families:
- Shared and private accounts
- Joint budgets
- Household spending categories

### Freelancer Mode
Business-focused features:
- Personal vs. business tagging
- Tax/VAT categories
- Receipt attachment
- Accountant export

The app understands Icelandic spending:

## Onboarding Flow

1. **Value explanation** — What the app does in simple words
2. **Trust screen** — Security explanation (no passwords stored, Open Banking, disconnect anytime)
3. **Connect bank** — Secure Open Banking or CSV upload fallback
4. **First insight moment** — Immediate "wow" screen with categories, recurring payments, and savings opportunities
